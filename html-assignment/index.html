About Me

My name is Ho Chit Au-Yeung. But you can call me Dorothy. I studied fine arts in HK. But somehow, I set foot on graphic design. I’ve been working as a full time graphic designer with several fashion brands for few years while also running my side business. I do freelance graphic design, illustration, as well as commissioned paintings. 🎨

My favourite hobby is watching movies, especially horror movies, and cult movies of any kind. It’s really stress relieving for me. Other than that, I love drawing. I usually use watercolour and ink. But recently I’ve switched to using iPad. And I like it. It’s convenient and the outcome is just amazing. 💖
